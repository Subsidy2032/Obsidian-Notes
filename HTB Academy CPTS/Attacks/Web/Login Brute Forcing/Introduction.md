A small list of files that can contain hashed passwords:

|**`Windows`**|**`Linux`**|
|---|---|
|unattend.xml|shadow|
|sysprep.inf|shadow.bak|
|SAM|password|

There are many tools and methods to utilize for login brute-forcing, like:

- `Ncrack`
- `wfuzz`
- `medusa`
- `patator`
- `hydra`
- and others.
